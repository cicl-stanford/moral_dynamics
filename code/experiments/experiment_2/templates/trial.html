Instructions

In this experiment, you will be shown interactions between creatures from the alien planet Gaya.

On planet Gaya there are two types of creatures: Blue and Green.

On Planet Gaya there are also Fireballs:

  • Fireballs can hurt Green creatures.
  • Fireballs cannot hurt Blue creatures.
  • Blues can see Fireballs.
  • Greens cannot see Fireballs.

In the experiment, we will show you 17 videos. In all videos, a Green creature is burned as a result of colliding with a Fireball.

Before beginning with the experiment, we will tell you a little more about planet Gaya.

Please click Start to begin.
