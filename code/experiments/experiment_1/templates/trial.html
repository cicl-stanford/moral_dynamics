Instructions

In this experiment, you will be shown interactions between creatures from the alien planet Gaya.

On planet Gaya there are two types of creatures: Blues and Greens.

On Planet Gaya there are also Fireballs:

  • Fireballs can hurt Greens.
  • Fireballs cannot hurt Blues.
  • Blues can see Fireballs.
  • Greens cannot see Fireballs.

In the experiment, we will show you 11 pairs of videos. In all videos, a Green is burned as a result of colliding with a Fireball.

Your task is to judge, for each pair, in which video was what the Blue did worse.

Before beginning with the experiment, we will tell you a little more about planet Gaya.
